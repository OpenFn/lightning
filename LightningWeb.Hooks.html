        on_mount(atom, arg2, session, socket)

        View Source

        Finds and assigns a project to the socket, if a user doesn't have access they are redirected and shown a 'No Access' screen via a :nav flash message.

        There is a fallthru function, when there is no project_id in the params - this is for liveviews that may or may not have a project_id depending on usage - like DashboardLive.
